---
title: "Switch workspaces"
description: "Move between workspaces. Each one is fully isolated."
---

If you have more than one workspace, you can switch between them at any time. Workspaces are fully isolated — nothing crosses between them unless you explicitly move or copy it.

## How to switch

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the workspace menu">
    Click your workspace name in the top-left corner of the desktop app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a workspace">
    Your workspaces are listed in the dropdown. Click the one you want to switch to.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You'll be taken into that workspace immediately. Everything you see — notes, collections, members, settings — belongs to that workspace only.

## Workspace isolation

This is worth understanding clearly:

- **Notes don't cross workspaces.** A note captured in Workspace A is not visible in Workspace B.
- **Search is scoped to the current workspace.** Searching in Workspace A will not surface results from Workspace B.
- **Bird is scoped to the current workspace.** When you ask Bird a question, it draws on notes from the workspace you're currently in.
- **Members are per workspace.** A colleague in Workspace A won't have access to Workspace B unless explicitly invited.

This isolation is intentional. It means you can use separate workspaces for different projects, clients, or contexts without any risk of information crossing over.

## Switching on mobile

Bower on your phone reflects your current workspace. To switch workspace on mobile:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap your avatar or workspace name in the top-left corner" />
  <Step title="Select the workspace you want to switch to" />
</Steps>

Capture on mobile always goes to your currently active workspace.
