Add colleagues to your workspace so they can capture, search, and collaborate on shared notes and collections.

## How to invite someone

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the workspace">
    Open the workspace you want to invite them to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Settings > Members" />
  <Step title="Click Invite member" />
  <Step title="Enter their email address" />
  <Step title="Choose their role">
    Select **Member** or **Admin**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Send invite">
    They'll receive an email with a link to join. If they don't have a Bower account yet, the link will prompt them to create one first.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Roles

| Role | What they can do |
| --- | --- |
| Member | Capture, view, edit, and search all notes in the workspace |
| Admin | Everything a Member can do, plus: invite and remove members, change workspace settings, manage billing |

## Pending invites

Invites that haven't been accepted yet appear under **Settings > Members > Pending invites**. You can resend or revoke a pending invite from there.

## What members can see

All members of a workspace can see all notes and collections in that workspace by default. If you need to restrict access to specific collections, use **Share settings** on that collection.

Members cannot see notes or collections in your other workspaces — workspaces are fully isolated from each other.

## Removing a member

To remove someone from the workspace, go to **Settings > Members**, find their name, and click **Remove**. Their notes remain in the workspace — only their access is revoked.
