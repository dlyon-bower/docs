Add colleagues to your workspace so they can capture, search, and collaborate on shared notes and collections.

## How to invite someone

There are two ways to invite someone:

**From the settings popover** — click your avatar in the bottom-left corner, then click **Invite member**.

**From workspace management** — go to **Settings > Workspace Management** and use the invite option there.

Both routes generate a shareable invite link. Copy it and send it to your colleague however you like — email, Slack, text message.

Invite links expire after **30 days**. If a link expires, generate a new one.

## Roles

| Role | What they can do |
| --- | --- |
| Member | Capture, view, edit, and search all notes in the workspace |
| Admin | Everything a Member can do, plus: remove members, change workspace settings, manage billing |

## Pending invites

Invites that haven't been accepted yet are visible in **Settings > Workspace Management**. You can revoke a pending invite or generate a new link from there.

## What members can see

All members of a workspace can see all notes and collections in that workspace by default. There are no per-collection access restrictions.

Members cannot see notes or collections in your other workspaces — workspaces are fully isolated from each other.

## Removing a member

To remove someone from the workspace, go to **Settings > Members**, find their name, and click **Remove**. Their notes remain in the workspace — only their access is revoked.
