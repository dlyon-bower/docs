title: "Manage your account"
description: "Profile, notifications, security, and account settings."
---

Update your profile name, workspace name, and other account settings from the Settings panel.

## Update your profile name

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click your avatar in the bottom-left corner of the desktop app" />
  <Step title="Select Profile settings" />
  <Step title="Edit your display name" />
  <Step title="Click Save" />
</Steps>

Your display name appears in notes you create, comments, and member lists in shared workspaces.

## Update your workspace name

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings > Workspace" />
  <Step title="Click the workspace name field" />
  <Step title="Edit the name" />
  <Step title="Click Save" />
</Steps>

Workspace names are visible to all members of that workspace. Changing the name doesn't affect any notes, collections, or member access.

## Change your email or password

Email and password changes are managed from **Settings > Account > Security**. You'll be asked to verify your current password before making changes.

## Delete your account

<Warning>
Account deletion is permanent. Your notes, collections, and workspaces are removed and cannot be recovered.
</Warning>

To delete your account, go to **Settings > Account > Delete account**. You'll be asked to confirm by typing your email address.

If you're the owner of a shared workspace, you'll need to transfer ownership to another admin before deleting your account.
