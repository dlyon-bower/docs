## View your current plan

Go to **Settings > Billing**. You'll see your current plan, billing cycle, next
billing date, and usage bars for storage, members, and owned workspaces. Click
**Compare all features** above the plan cards to see the full matrix.

<Note>
The Billing tab is only visible to workspace owners and admins.
</Note>

## Upgrade your plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings > Billing" />
  <Step title="Click Monthly or Annual above the plan cards" />
  <Step title="Choose Pro or Team" />
  <Step title="Complete payment via Stripe Checkout" />
</Steps>

All paid plans include a 14-day free trial — no credit card required to start.
You can pay with a credit or debit card. After payment (or during the trial),
your workspace is upgraded immediately — no need to sign out or refresh.

## Manage your subscription

Click **Manage Billing** in the Billing tab to open the Stripe Customer Portal.
From there you can:

- Change your plan (upgrade or downgrade)
- Switch between monthly and annual billing
- Update your payment method
- View past invoices and receipts
- Cancel your subscription

<Note>
Upgrades take effect immediately with a prorated charge. Downgrades take effect
at the end of your current billing period — you keep paid features until then.
</Note>

## What happens if payment fails

If a payment fails, your workspace enters a **past due** state. You'll see a
warning banner in the Billing tab asking you to update your payment method.
Stripe automatically retries failed payments several times over the following
weeks. If all retries fail, your workspace is downgraded to the Starter plan.

While in past-due state, your existing data is preserved — nothing is deleted.
Features gated behind your paid plan may become restricted until payment is
resolved.

## Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time from the Stripe Customer Portal
(Settings > Billing > Manage Billing). After cancellation:

- You keep access to your paid plan until the end of the current billing period
- At period end, your workspace moves to the Starter plan
- Your data is preserved — nothing is deleted
- Storage, member, workspace, and audit-retention limits revert to Starter levels
- Public share links will no longer be accessible

You can reactivate any time before period end from the same portal to keep your
plan.

## Student discount

Students and researchers at accredited institutions can request a free Pro
plan. Click **Free for students** under the Pro card on the Billing tab to get
the contact details — we review requests manually.

## Workspace deletion and billing

If you delete a workspace that has an active subscription, the subscription is
cancelled automatically. No further charges will occur.
