A real-time conversation with Bird, with full video awareness. Describe what you're seeing, show your screen or camera, and get contextual assistance without breaking your flow.

## What live agent mode is

Live agent mode is a continuous, low-latency audio and video session with Bird. Unlike the standard chat interface — where you type a question and wait for a response — live mode is conversational. You can talk through an experiment, describe what's on an instrument display, or ask follow-up questions without stopping to type.

With video awareness enabled, Bird can see what your camera sees and respond to visual context: "What's the reading on that display?" or "Does this sample look contaminated to you?" are reasonable things to ask.

## When to use it

- You're mid-experiment and need hands-free assistance
- You want to think out loud and have Bird track the key observations
- You need to describe something visual that would be difficult to type
- You want a real-time second opinion on an observation before moving to the next step

## How to start a live session

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Live mode">
    In the Bower desktop app, click **Live** in the top navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your mode">
    Select **Audio only** or **Audio + Video**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Allow access">
    Allow microphone (and camera, if video) access when prompted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start talking">
    Bird is listening.
  </Step>
</Steps>

To end the session, click **End session**. A summary of the conversation is saved automatically as a note in your workspace.

## Video awareness

With video enabled, Bird processes your camera feed in real time. It can:

- Read values from instrument displays you point the camera at
- Describe what it sees when asked
- Flag visual anomalies if you ask it to watch for them

Bird will not act on visual information unprompted — it responds when you ask.

## What's saved

At the end of every live session, Bower saves:

- A full transcript of the conversation
- A summary note with key observations Bird identified
- Any values or data points Bird extracted during the session

All of this is searchable immediately after the session ends.
