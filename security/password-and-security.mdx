## Sign-in methods

Bower supports two ways to sign in:

- **Email and password** — create an account with your email address and a password.
- **Google** — sign in with your Google account. No separate password needed.

You can see which sign-in methods are connected to your account in **Settings > Account > Security**.

## Changing your password

1. Go to **Settings > Account > Security**.
2. Enter your current password.
3. Enter your new password.
4. Click **Save**.

## Resetting a forgotten password

If you can't sign in:

1. On the sign-in page, click **Forgot password**.
2. Enter your email address.
3. Check your inbox for a password reset link.
4. Click the link and set a new password.

The reset link expires after a limited time. If it's expired, request a new one.

## Signing out

To sign out, go to **Settings > Account > Security** and click **Sign out**. This signs you out of the current device only — other sessions remain active.

## Account security tips

- Use a strong, unique password that you don't reuse elsewhere.
- If you use Google sign-in, your account security depends on your Google account — enable two-factor authentication there.
- If you suspect unauthorised access, change your password immediately and check the [audit logs](/security/audit-logs) for unexpected activity.
