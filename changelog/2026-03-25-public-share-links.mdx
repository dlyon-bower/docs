**Shipped 2026-03-25 · Permissions**

Sometimes you need to send a note to someone outside your workspace — a
collaborator at another institution, a journal reviewer, a thesis advisor —
without setting up shared workspace access. Public share links cover that
case.

## What's new

- **Generate a public link** for any note, attachment, or collection.
  Recipients open the link in their browser; no Bower account needed.
- **Read-only by default** — the link grants view access only. The original
  owner keeps full control.
- **Revocable any time** — delete the link from the share dialog and the
  URL stops working immediately.
- **Annotation previews carry through** — public viewers see your inline
  annotations on images, not just the raw originals.

## Privacy

Public links are unlisted — they aren't indexed, listed, or discoverable
unless you share the URL. They include a long random token, so guessing one
isn't realistic. Treat the URL as the password.
