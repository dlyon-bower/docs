**Shipped 2026-03-08 · Brand**

Bower has a new look. The interface should feel like a Victorian scientific
plate — precise, generous with whitespace, confident in its typography. We've
taken a step closer to that.

## What changed

- **Typography** — DM Sans for body copy, Playfair Display for headings. The
  pairing is grounded and academic without being austere.
- **Colour palette** — a refined accent system that reads cleanly in both
  light and dark mode, with stronger contrast on the surfaces that matter
  (artifact titles, action buttons, status banners).
- **Logo and favicon** — new mark across the app, browser tab, mobile home
  screen, and email comms.

## Why this matters

The previous look was workmanlike. This one matches the kind of work you do
in Bower: careful, considered, and scientific. Same product, sharper presence.
