**Shipped 2026-04-08 · Workspace**

Hard deletes are stressful. You've all done that thing where you delete the
wrong note, and it takes a second for your stomach to drop. Trash fixes
that — every delete is now recoverable.

## What's new

- **Soft delete** — deleting an artifact moves it to the trash rather than
  removing it. The artifact disappears from your workspace immediately, but
  it's recoverable.
- **Trash page** — a dedicated view in the sidebar lists deleted artifacts
  with the date deleted and the person who did it.
- **One-click restore** — restore a single artifact, or use the floating
  toolbar to restore many at once.
- **Undo toast** — right after a delete, a toast appears with an Undo
  button. One click and the deletion is reversed without a trip to the
  trash.
- **Scheduled purge** — items older than 30 days are permanently removed
  by a background job, so trash doesn't grow forever.

## Why this matters

Soft deletes change the cost of cleanup. You can confidently tidy a noisy
collection knowing nothing is gone forever within the recovery window.

## Try it

Find the **Trash** entry in your sidebar to see what's been deleted
recently, or wait for the undo toast next time you delete something.
