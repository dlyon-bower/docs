## April 2026

<Update label="2026-04-30" description="Private Mode + workspace owner gates · Permissions">
  {/* TODO(design): hero at /images/changelog/private-mode-hero.png */}

  ### Private workspaces, by default if you want them

  Workspace owners can flip an entire workspace into **Private Mode**, so
  every artifact you create starts visible only to you and you choose what
  to share. A new **Owner** role centralises admin-only controls for
  permissions, members, and workspace settings.

  [Read more →](/changelog/2026-04-30-private-mode)
</Update>

<Update label="2026-04-27" description="Notion-style sharing on every artifact · Permissions">

  ### Share any note or attachment, without sharing your whole workspace

  Every note, attachment, collection, and project now has its own share
  dialog — invite collaborators with **View**, **Edit**, or **Manage**
  access at the artifact level.

  [Read more →](/changelog/2026-04-27-notion-style-sharing)
</Update>

<Update label="2026-04-26" description="Inferred relationships · AI">
  {/* TODO(design): hero at /images/changelog/inferred-relationships-hero.png */}

  Bird now reads your notes in the background and suggests links to related

  chips on the note view make jumping between related work a single click.

  [Read more →](/changelog/2026-04-26-inferred-relationships)
</Update>
