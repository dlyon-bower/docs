**Shipped 2026-04-24 · Capture**

Lab notebooks have always had a weak spot: the moment you regret an edit, you
can't get the previous version back. Bower now keeps a full version history
of every note, with a viewer that makes diffs legible.

## What's new

- **Automatic versioning** — every meaningful save creates a version. No
  manual "save snapshot" needed.
- **Side-by-side diff viewer** — pick any two versions and see exactly what
  changed: text, attachments, annotations, tags, links.
- **One-click restore** — restore any prior version. The restore itself is
  a new version, so you can roll forward again if you change your mind.
- **Author attribution** — every version shows who made it and when.
- **Annotations restore alongside content** — restoring a version brings back
  the drawings on inline images, not just the text.

## Try it

Open any note → **⋮ menu → Version history**.
