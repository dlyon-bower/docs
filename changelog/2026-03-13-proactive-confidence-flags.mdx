**Shipped 2026-03-13 · AI**

Voice transcription and OCR usually work well. The risk is when they
*almost* work — a misread digit, a mangled unit, a transcription that
looks plausible but isn't. Bird now reads every capture after the fact,
scores its confidence, and flags any that look uncertain.

## What's new

- **Background quality check** after every voice transcription and
  every photo OCR.
- **Confidence scoring** — Bird scores the result against the source
  (audio recording or original image) and assigns high, medium, or low
  confidence.
- **Proactive toast** — if confidence is medium or low, Bird surfaces a
  short notification on your screen pointing you at the specific
  ambiguous values, with a one-click jump to the note.
- **No interruption to capture** — the check runs after the capture is
  saved. It never blocks you from finishing what you were doing.

## Why this matters

Silent errors are the worst kind. A typo you can see is fixable. A
misread "240 µmol/L" that looks the same as "240 µmol/I" only surfaces
months later when someone tries to reproduce the result. Proactive
confidence flags catch the ambiguous cases at the moment of capture,
when the source is fresh and the fix is cheap.
