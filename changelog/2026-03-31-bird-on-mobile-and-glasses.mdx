**Shipped 2026-03-31 · Live Mode**

Bird should be where your hands are. Two updates extend Live Mode beyond the
desktop.

## Bird on mobile

A dedicated Bird page is now available on mobile at `/m/bird`. Open it from
the mobile sidebar and you get the full Bird chat experience tuned for
thumbs — same workspace knowledge, same live sessions, same web search.

A floating action button on the chat starts a Live Mode session with one
tap when you need real-time conversation rather than turn-by-turn chat.

## Bird on Ray-Ban Meta glasses

Live Mode now supports Ray-Ban Meta glasses as a capture platform. Connect
the glasses to your Bower workspace and:

- Bird sees what you see — the wearer's first-person camera feeds Live
  Mode in real time.
- High-resolution photo capture — when you ask Bird to take a still, the
  glasses upload a high-res frame rather than a downsampled video frame,
  for cleaner OCR and visual analysis.
- The same wake-word standby model applies — Bird stays quiet until you
  call it.

## Why this matters

The bench is the place where capture matters most, and it's the place where
keyboards and screens are least practical. Mobile and glasses meet you where
the work is happening.
