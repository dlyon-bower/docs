**Shipped 2026-04-20 · Trust & compliance**

The audit log is the source of truth for "who did what to which artifact."
We've rebuilt it as a chronological timeline that reads cleanly without
losing any of the underlying detail.

## What's new

- **Timeline layout** — a chronological feed grouped by day, with each
  event showing actor, action, and target.
- **Multi-select filters** — filter by user, action type, entity type, and
  date range, with chip-based selections that combine cleanly.
- **Search** — full-text search across all events and metadata, useful
  when you remember an artifact name but not the date.
- **Login deduplication** — repeated logins from the same session no
  longer flood the timeline; they collapse into a single rolled-up event.
- **Richer event details** — for state-changing events (create, update,
  delete), the changed fields and their old/new values are shown inline
  for workspace members to review.
- **Cursor pagination + export** — fast scrolling through long histories
  and full CSV export for compliance archives.

## Try it

Open the **Audit log** entry in your sidebar (or **Settings → Trust →
Audit logs** depending on your role).
