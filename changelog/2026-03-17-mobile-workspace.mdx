**Shipped 2026-03-17 · Mobile**

Lab capture happens on your phone, but until now everything *after* capture
required a desktop. The mobile workspace is now full-featured: read, search,
edit, organise, and chat with Bird from your phone.

## What's new

- **Mobile-first routes** — every page in Bower has a responsive layout
  tuned for one-handed use. Read a note on the bus. Reorganise a collection
  while waiting for a centrifuge.
- **Mobile search** — the global search modal works the same way on mobile
  as on desktop, with infinite-scroll pagination through results.
- **Capture-first home screen** — voice and photo capture cards stay one
  tap away from anywhere in the app.
- **Editor on mobile** — the rich text editor adapts to thumb typing, with
  the slash-command menu reachable from a toolbar button.

## Try it

Open [app.bowerlabs.ai](https://app.bowerlabs.ai) on your phone. For a
native-feeling launch experience, [add Bower to your home
screen](/getting-started/install-pwa).
