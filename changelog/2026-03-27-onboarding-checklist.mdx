**Shipped 2026-03-27 · Workspace**

Most onboarding tours throw twenty things at a new user and hope something
sticks. We've tried for the opposite — a short checklist of the steps that
have been observed to actually predict whether someone gets value out of
Bower in their first week.

## What's new

A new sidebar entry shows your onboarding progress as a circular ring. Open
it to see the checklist:

- Make your first capture (voice, photo, or text)
- Try mobile capture
- Create a collection
- Run a Bird query
- Invite a teammate
- Seed your workspace with a few existing files

Each step is dismissable, and the whole panel can be hidden once you've
found your rhythm.

## Why this matters

We tracked which steps correlate with sustained week-2 use, then built the
checklist around those. It's not exhaustive — it's load-bearing.
