## 2. What data we collect

**Account data** (collected at signup):
- Email address
- Name (optional)
- Authentication credentials (managed by Firebase Authentication)

**Workspace data** (created through use of the service):
- Research notes, documents, protocols
- Uploaded files (images, audio, PDFs, documents)
- Collections and project structures
- Tags and metadata
- AI chat conversations with Bird

**Automatically collected data:**
- IP address (hashed in logs, stored in audit logs)
- Browser user agent (stored in audit logs)
- Device type (mobile/desktop)
- Pages viewed and feature usage (only with [analytics consent](/trust-centre/cookies))

## 4. AI data processing

When you use Bower's AI features (Bird chat, voice transcription, OCR, document processing), your content is sent to AI model providers for processing.

- **Your data is not used to train AI models.** All providers are configured with zero-retention or inference-only data processing.
- AI processing is necessary to provide the features you use.
- You choose when to use AI features — they are not applied automatically without your action.

## 8. Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

| Right | How to Exercise |
|-------|----------------|
| **Access** (Art. 15) | Export all your data from Settings > Privacy > Export my data |
| **Rectification** (Art. 16) | Update your profile in Settings > Account |
| **Erasure** (Art. 17) | Delete your account from Settings > Account > Delete account |
| **Data Portability** (Art. 20) | Export data as JSON (machine-readable format) |
| **Object** (Art. 21) | Contact [privacy@bowerlabs.ai](mailto:privacy@bowerlabs.ai) |
| **Restrict Processing** (Art. 18) | Contact [privacy@bowerlabs.ai](mailto:privacy@bowerlabs.ai) |
| **Withdraw Consent** (Art. 7(3)) | Update cookie preferences in Settings > Privacy |

## 10. Security

We implement technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

- Encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
- Workspace-level data isolation at the database layer
- Role-based access control (Owner, Admin, Member, Viewer)
- Audit logging of all data access and modifications
- Automatic session timeout after 30 minutes of inactivity
- All secrets stored in Google Secret Manager

See our [Data Privacy](/trust-centre/data-privacy) page for full technical details.

## 11. Children's privacy

Bower is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact [privacy@bowerlabs.ai](mailto:privacy@bowerlabs.ai).
