When you sign up for Bower, a short setup flow gets you into your workspace in under a minute.

## The onboarding steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up with your email or Google account at [app.bowerlabs.ai](https://app.bowerlabs.ai).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your name">
    Enter your display name. This appears on notes you create and in workspace member lists.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create or join a workspace">
    If you signed up on your own, you'll create a new workspace — give it a name (your lab, project, or research group).

    If you followed an invitation link, you'll be taken directly into the workspace that invited you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start capturing">
    You're in. The dashboard shows your quick capture options — voice, camera, import, or type. Everything else is available from the sidebar.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After setup

- Your workspace starts empty. Capture your first note or [import existing files](/capture/batch-import).
- [Invite team members](/workspaces-and-account/invite-team-members) if you want to collaborate.
- All features — Bird, search, collections, protocols — are available immediately.
- You can [create additional workspaces](/workspaces-and-account/create-workspace) at any time.
